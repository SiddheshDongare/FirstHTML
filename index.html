Rick & Morty

A look into the daily exploits of a super scientist and his not-so-bright grandson.

An animated series on Adult-swim & Netflix about the infinite adventures of Rick, a genius alcoholic and careless scientist, with his grandson Morty, a 14 year-old anxious boy who is not so smart. Together, they explore the infinite universes; causing mayhem and running into trouble.
